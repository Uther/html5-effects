Hover over the fingers to move the puppets or use the following keys

Puppet one:

  • A = Left leg
  • S = Left arm
  • E = Head
  • D = Right arm
  • F = Right leg

Puppet two:

  • H = Left leg
  • J = Left arm
  • U = Head
  • K = Right arm
  • L = Right leg
